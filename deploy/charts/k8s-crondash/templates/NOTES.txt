k8s-crondash has been installed!

Get the dashboard URL by running:

  export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods -l "app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
  kubectl port-forward "$POD_NAME" 3000:3000

Then open http://localhost:3000 in your browser.

Alternatively, use the service:

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "k8s-crondash.name" . }} {{ .Values.service.port }}:{{ .Values.service.targetPort }}

Then open http://localhost:{{ .Values.service.port }} in your browser.
